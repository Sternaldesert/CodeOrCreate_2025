Your journey to Nature is only a bus ride away

The LTD, (Lane Transit District) is a specialized district/program that has provided transportation and reliable service to Lane County since 1970. In and around the Eugene and Springfield area, there are plenty of recreational parks and trails that are easily accessible by bus - Eugene won’t leave you hanging in the spirit of adventure!

Hiking Trails

LTD offers an array of bus routes that can bring you to diverse, nature-filled trails right in the wilderness - perfect for outdoor lovers wanting to take a jog or a slow-paced walk alongside the flowers and ferns.

Bike Paths

Want to go biking instead? LTD can also drive you to local bike paths around your area! From the Rosa Parks Path to Fern Ridge, if you’ve got wheels - Eugene’s got your back.

Community Parks

A relaxing walk in the park can help remind us about the simple things in life, and LTD can further help you by providing transportation to get to these places of zen. Alton Baker, Hendricks, Washburne too - none of them are safe from the guiding hand of these buses.
